About Will Givens

Will Givens is a decorated poker professional with a career spanning over a decade, amassing over $3.3 million in live tournament earnings. His journey is marked by an unwavering dedication to the game and a consistent ability to perform at the highest level. From the high-stakes tables in Las Vegas to major tours across the United States, Will has demonstrated the skill, discipline, and strategic mindset required to dominate the felt, making him an ideal coach for players looking to elevate their game.

Career Highlights

Will's impressive track record includes a multitude of deep runs, final table appearances, and outright victories against some of the world's best players.


Largest Cash: A remarkable 2nd place finish in a **WSOP $600 Deep Stack Championship**, earning him **$245,606**. This cash highlights his ability to navigate massive fields and compete for poker's most prestigious hardware.


WPT Finalist: A 6th place finish at the **WPT Venetian Main Event**, a high-profile final table performance that secured a cash of **$87,760**. He also took 4th place at the **WPT Maryland Main Event**, cashing for **$121,112**.


Consistent Wins: He has racked up numerous other 1st place finishes in major circuits, including several **WSOP Circuit** events, the **Wynn Millions Poker Series**, and the **WPT Borgata Winter Poker Open**.
