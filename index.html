Our Coaching Services

Will's coaching is less about rigid rules and more about developing a deep, intuitive feel for the game. We focus on teaching you how to think like an elite pro, not just memorize charts. Our curriculum and services are designed to build a complete player.

Core Content and Curriculum

  • Advanced Hand Reading and Opponent Analysis

    Learn how to deduce opponent's holdings and thought processes by analyzing physical and online tells, betting patterns, and constructing accurate hand ranges.

  • The "Mental Game" and "Table Presence"

    Master emotional control, avoid tilt, and use your table image to manipulate opponents. This unique focus is a cornerstone of becoming a truly great player.

  • Deep Stack and High-Stakes Strategy

    Delve into the complex, multi-street strategies required in high-stakes cash games, including creative bet-sizing, thin value betting, and advanced bluffing.

  • Exploitative Play and Deviating from GTO

    Go beyond Game Theory Optimal (GTO) play. Learn how to identify and exploit weaknesses in your opponents to maximize your edge against them.

  • Hand Histories and Real-Life Examples

    Gain direct insight into Will's thought process by breaking down iconic and lesser-known hands he has played in crucial moments.
